ABOUT US

Structure before the spotlight.

NOWAYY is a boutique consultancy built to support women in sport with strategy, protection, and long-term vision.

We exist to do the work that often happens too late, building structure early so careers, platforms, and partnerships are designed to last.

We are sport-led, culture-driven, and ownership-focused.

Our work is grounded in cultural study and expressed through consulting, representation, events, and cultural activations.

Culture is not an add-on to what we do. It is the lens through which every decision is made.

Our Role

Women in sport are more visible than ever, yet often under-supported. Too much exposure. Too little structure. Short-term wins with long-term consequences.

Our role is to change that.

We build the structures that support and protect women in sport. This includes shaping careers, guiding representation, and aligning the brands, businesses, and cultural platforms that surround them, particularly women-owned ventures.

What We Do

Athlete Development & Representation

Career strategy and representation for women athletes and athlete-creators across sport, media, and culture.

Cultural & Brand Alignment

Strategic alignment between talent, brands, and cultural moments, ensuring partnerships add value, not noise.

Business & Ownership Support

Narrative, cultural, and growth strategy for women-owned ventures that contribute to long-term ecosystems.
